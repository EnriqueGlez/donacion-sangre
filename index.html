Requirements

To donate blood, please meet the following requirements:

  • Age: 18-65 years
  • Weight: At least 50 kg
  • No recent infections

Steps to Donate

Follow these steps to donate:

  1. Find a donation center.
  2. Bring your ID.
  3. Complete the donation form.
  4. Undergo a health screening.
  5. Donate blood!
