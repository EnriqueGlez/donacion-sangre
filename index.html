Patient Data

Every donation is vital for patients in need of blood transfusions. We collect data to ensure safe and efficient matches.

Requirements

  • Must be at least 18 years old.
  • Weigh at least 110 pounds (50 kg).
  • Be in good health.
  • No recent tattoos or piercings.

Steps to Donate

  1. Find a donation center.
  2. Fill out a donor registration form.
  3. Undergo a brief health screening.
  4. Make your donation.
  5. Enjoy refreshments afterward!
